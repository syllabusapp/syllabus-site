Serving students, teachers and administrators.

The ultimate purpose for Syllabus is to help students, teachers and administrators have a better online schooling experience. By creating a sophisticated and easy to use system, Syllabus helps any college, university or seminar create an entire online school with ease.

Platform Roadmap

Syllabus is in the early stages of design & development. In order to complete and fulfill the demand for a more modern LMS, a specific roadmap must be followed. This roadmap shows what phases have been complete, what is currently being work on and what is coming up.

  • Complete

    Conceptualization

    Syllabus began by thinking through what online schools needed most. This was distilled down into a basic feature list of which this site is made of. To learn more about the Syllabus concept, simply browse the additional apages in this site.

  • In-progress

    Design & Front-end Development

    Creation of a full design system for Syllabus and front-end templates is currently underway. This requires several phases of research, wireframes, static designs and front-end prototypes. Syllabus is currently working with respected universities during the research phase.

  • Coming Up

    Full System Development

    This is also known as "connecting the dots." Since the majority of work will be complete by this stage, the only remaining piece is to get a developer to create the appropriate databases, set up the correct servers and prepare the system to be used.
