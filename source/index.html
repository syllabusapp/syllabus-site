The first LMS built exclusively with higher education in mind.

Syllabus is a complete LMS for small to medium sized colleges and universities. It focuses on clarity and simplicity for administrators, teachers and students. With Syllabus, any school can have entire online degree programs.

Who is Syllabus built for?

Students

With Syllabus, students can not only take full-length, accredited courses from their school of choice, but they can also manage their registration, tuition and other miscellaneous items from within their account settings.

Administrators

Because students manage their own accounts, no more unneccessary paperwork or time spent reviewing errors. Syllabus takes care of it all so that you have time to focus on more important issues.

Teachers
